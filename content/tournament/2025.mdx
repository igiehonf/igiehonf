### Eligibility Criteria
- Open to all Senior Secondary Schools in Edo State.  
- Participants must be current Senior Secondary Students.  
- Teams consist of 2 students (any class/department).  
- Each team must have a teacher coach.  
- Written test + Oral finals (top 8 schools).
